MISSION

Life Primer is a way to help you get things done—big things.

Life Primer is not a to-do list organizer. We don't want to help you manage your everyday tasks like laundry and getting your boots repaired and calling your mom. (We do want you to call your mom, though. Finish reading this and then go call your mom!) Life Primer is about things bigger than that, the things that might take years to accomplish, because those are the tough things to get done. We want to make it easier.

"I should take some rock climbing lessons...and then travel to Africa and climb Mt. Kilimanjaro"

We all have Kilimanjaro dreams and we all talk about those dreams, but too often do we keep them in a dreamy state in our minds and never weave them into our real lives where we actually do things. Life Primer is about doing. By linking your list items to local, national, and international happenings that match your goals, we want to help you take action. For our climber dreamer up there, we might let him know of any outdoor sports classes offered in his city or notify him when flights to Tanzania go on sale. Our goal is to help you meet your goals all while tracking your progress along the way and sharing your actions with the people you care about and care to share with.
